15+ Years of Taste

Serving Tradition Since 2010

Aman Panjabi Restaurant is a household name in Bundi for anyone seeking authentic North Indian and Punjabi cuisine. Our journey started with a simple goal: to serve food that tastes like home.

We take pride in our hygiene standards and use only the finest spices sourced directly from local markets. Whether it's our Dal Makhani simmered overnight or our crisp Butter Naans, quality is never compromised.
